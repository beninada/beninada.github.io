About Me

I’m a User Experience Designer at NASA’s Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL) and a Computer Science undergrad at UC San Diego. I live in Pasadena, CA, but I’ll be moving back to school in October to complete my degree by June 2015. Learning, making, and spreading good design is my passion.

Although most of my time is spent building the next greatest interfaces for architecting spacecraft missions at JPL, I do delve into my own side projects as well. You can check out the design work that I’ve done for these projects on my Behance portfolio. I am currently an open freelancer, so if you’d like to discuss something I can help you design & build, feel free to send me an email. I’m always open to a chat about User Experience, Usability, Typography, as well as just good design in general.
